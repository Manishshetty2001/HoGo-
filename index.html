Our Story

Born in 2023, HoGo Fresh represents a new era in India's agricultural ecosystem. We recognized an opportunity to transform how food moves from farms to tables across our nation.

We're pioneering India's first integrated agricultural network - a seamless bridge between hardworking farmers and quality-conscious buyers. Our technology platform creates an optimized ecosystem where every participant thrives.

Today, HoGo Fresh operates across multiple states in India, serving thousands of farmers and businesses with our innovative approach to agricultural logistics.
